## What Might NOT Work Well:

### 1. **The Point System & Gamification**
- **Issue**: Points for tasks might create anxiety for people with ADHD who already struggle with task completion guilt
- **Alternative**: Focus on progress tracking without numerical scores; use visual progress indicators instead

### 2. **Manual Health Data Entry**
- **Issue**: People with ADHD will forget to enter data manually; 0 values skipping fields adds cognitive load
- **Alternative**: Auto-sync with wearables or simplify to just 1-2 most important metrics

### 3. **Fixed Daily Rotation Tasks**
- **Issue**: "Day 7 Focus" with fixed times (Morning/Evening) doesn't accommodate irregular ADHD schedules
- **Alternative**: Allow flexible timing with gentle reminders rather than fixed schedules

### 4. **Nutrition Plan Structure**
- **Issue**: Three-phase plan with specific foods might be overwhelming for decision-fatigued users
- **Alternative**: Simplified "brain food" suggestions with easy recipes
