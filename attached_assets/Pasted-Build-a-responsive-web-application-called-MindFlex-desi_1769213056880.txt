Build a responsive web application called “MindFlex” designed to support ADHD patients in managing daily tasks through playful activities, relaxation tools, and progress tracking. The site should be engaging, accessible, and privacy‑respecting.



 Purpose
- Help ADHD patients structure their day with **small, achievable tasks**.  
- Provide **mini‑games, yoga, meditation, and music** to reduce stress and improve focus.  
- Track progress with **points, badges, and streaks**.  
- Offer **non‑diagnostic assessments** (AI webcam + questionnaire) to personalize routines.  
- Integrate **wearable data** (smartwatch) for future insights.



 Home Page (Section 1 modules)
This section is shown as per the UI/UX design.

Games: 
  - Simple mini‑games (reaction tapper, memory match, breathing rhythm).  
  - Purpose: redirect attention, reduce overthinking.  

Yoga:
  - Videos, images, and step‑by‑step explanations.  
  - Gentle stretches only, with safety notes.  

Music:
  - Sleep/relaxation playlists.  
  - Features: timer, volume control, loop option.  

Chatbot: 
  - Supportive, non‑clinical chat.  
  - Helps reduce stress and reframe thoughts.  



Main Features

1. AI Webcam Test (Assessment A)
- Patient answers questions on screen.  
- AI analyzes **facial engagement signals** (not medical diagnosis).  
- Data stored securely for personalization.  
- Consent required before camera access.  

 2. Questionnaire Test (Assessment B)
- Patient answers structured questions (focus, sleep, mood).  
- AI evaluates responses to suggest routines.  
- Combined with Assessment A → generates **Personalized Routine Recommendation**.  

 3. Smartwatch Data Integration
- Upload CSV/JSON with steps, sleep, heart rate averages.  
- Show **trends and progress charts**.  
- “Coming Soon”: automatic sync with Health Connect/Apple Health/Google Fit.  


 Core System Design

- Points & Rewards:
  - Earn points for completing tasks.  
  - Unlock badges and streaks.  

- **Profile & Progress:**  
  - Dashboard with charts, summaries, and badges.  
  - Weekly streak tracker.  

- **Accessibility:**  
  - Dyslexia‑friendly fonts, captions, reduced motion toggle.  
  - Large tap targets, high contrast mode.  

- **Privacy & Consent:**  
  - Clear data controls (export/delete).  
  - Camera/mic only with explicit consent.  

---

 Technical Blueprint

- **Frontend:** React + TypeScript, TailwindCSS.  
- **Backend:** Node.js + PostgreSQL.  
- **Auth:** OAuth/email login.  
- **Media:** HLS for music, CDN for videos/images.  
- **AI:**  
  - Webcam analysis: MediaPipe/TF.js (local processing).  
  - Questionnaire: rules engine for recommendations.  
- **Data Model:**  
  - User, Task, Session, Assessment, DeviceData, Badge.
